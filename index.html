Who I Am

About Me

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate from BRAC University, currently working as Junior Executive — Project Management at BRACNet Limited, where I coordinate telecoms infrastructure project schedules, track contract agreements, and support performance reporting.

My technical background spans Power Systems Analysis, Renewable Energy, and IoT. I completed 24-hour industry-grade DIgSILENT PowerFactory training and designed a real-site 1.89 kWp solar PV system using PVsyst, achieving 81.65% Performance Ratio.

As a UN Millennium Fellow (2025) and Aspire Leaders alumnus (Harvard-founded institute), I combine engineering rigour with leadership and a passion for sustainable development. Beyond engineering, I am an active singer and co-founder of Utkontha — a creative platform for music and performing arts — reflecting my belief that creativity and analytical thinking go hand in hand.
