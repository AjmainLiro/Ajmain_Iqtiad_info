Research · Presented at BEAR Summit 2025 · BRAC University
HRES Optimisation for Dublar Char — Metaheuristic Algorithm Comparison
First-ever system-level HRES optimisation for Dublar Char, Bangladesh. Custom MATLAB model simulating 8,760 hours of solar, wind, battery, and diesel dispatch using NASA POWER real climate data. SOA achieved lowest cost (USD 180,960 — 1 solar, 23 wind, 4 battery, 34.34 diesel); DE most stable; PSO fastest convergence. Demonstrates near-100% renewable reliability for an off-grid coastal community.

Published Paper · IAC-23,E5,4,8,x79313 · Baku, Azerbaijan · 2023
Portable Satellite Ground Station for Disaster Management in Bangladesh
Physically built and deployed a portable solar-powered satellite ground station to receive NOAA weather satellite imagery for disaster management in Bangladesh. Constructed and tested three antennas: V-Dipole, Double-Crossed Dipole (8 copper pipes), and QFH (Quadrifilar Helicoidal on PVC mast). Tracked NOAA-15, 18, 19 at 137 MHz using RTL-SDR + Raspberry Pi 4. Decoded real weather images via SDRSharp and WXtoImg. First-authored at the 74th IAC, Baku 2023.

Electronic Circuits II (EEE308) · BRAC University · Spring 2024
RTD-Based Digital Thermometer — Wheatstone Bridge & Difference Amplifier
Designed a precision digital thermometer using an RTD in a Wheatstone bridge (R = 10kΩ, VREF = 10V, α = 0.002/°C). Bridge output (0–0.5V across 0–100°C) amplified to 0–5V using a difference amplifier with buffer (Av = 11, R1 = 9kΩ, R2 = 100kΩ). Simulated in Proteus 8 across 11 temperature points — simulation closely matched theoretical values. Two alternative topologies evaluated and rejected.
